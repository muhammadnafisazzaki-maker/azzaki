About

I think in systems.

Get in touch →

I've always been drawn to work that sits between disciplines. Throughout my journey, I've worked across product management, digital marketing, HR, business operations, research, design, and creative projects. That variety has given me a broader perspective on how different parts of a business connect and how good ideas become real outcomes.

My work has ranged from delivering product features with development teams, recruiting and building operational systems, managing e-commerce catalogues, conducting competitor research, creating brand identities, and helping businesses communicate their value more effectively. Regardless of the project, my focus remains the same: understand the problem, find a practical solution, and execute it well. I enjoy working on challenges that require both strategic thinking and hands-on execution.

Today, I'm primarily open to freelance projects and collaborative opportunities. Whether you need help with marketing, research, branding, content, websites, business documentation, product strategy, or a combination of several disciplines, I'm always interested in meaningful work with ambitious people. If you're building something interesting and looking for someone who can adapt quickly, learn fast, and contribute across multiple functions, I'd love to hear from you.
